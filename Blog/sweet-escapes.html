Sweet Escapes: Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake

Indulge your sweet tooth with our Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake recipe. The perfect marriage of a rich, gooey center and a delicate outer layer...

  • Ingredients: Chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour
  • Baking Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Baking Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Whisk in sugar, eggs, and flour.
  3. Bake until the outer layer is set, and the center remains gooey.
