Kitchen Chronicles: Mastering the Art of Sourdough

Embark on the journey of sourdough mastery as we demystify the art of crafting the perfect loaf. From creating a robust starter to achieving that coveted crust and airy crumb...

  • Ingredients: Flour, water, salt, sourdough starter
  • Preparation Time: 1 week (including starter preparation)
  • Baking Time: 1 hour

Instructions

  1. Create a sourdough starter by combining flour and water and allowing it to ferment.

  2. Mix the starter with flour, water, and salt to form the dough.

  3. Allow the dough to rise and ferment, then bake to perfection.
