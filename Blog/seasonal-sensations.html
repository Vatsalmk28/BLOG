Seasonal Sensations: Fall-Inspired Pumpkin Risotto

Celebrate the flavors of fall with our Fall-Inspired Pumpkin Risotto recipe. Creamy Arborio rice meets the earthy sweetness of pumpkin in this comforting dish...

  • Ingredients: Arborio rice, pumpkin, onion, garlic, Parmesan cheese
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Cooking Instructions

  1. Saut√© onion and garlic, then add Arborio rice.
  2. Add diced pumpkin and gradually stir in warm broth until rice is cooked.
  3. Finish with Parmesan cheese and serve hot.
