1.Generative Ai

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that creates new and realistic content, such as text or images, by learning from existing data. It often involves the use of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), where a generator creates content and a discriminator evaluates its authenticity. Applications include art generation, text completion, and image synthesis. Ethical considerations are important in addressing potential misuse and unintended biases in generated content.
